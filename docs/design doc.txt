- Use It or Lose It

Premise
=======
This is a 3D platformer score attack game. Your goal is to find some kind of "goal object" in the level and bring it back to your starting point. Along the way, you can collect power-ups that grant you special abilities to get around faster (e.g., a jetpack) and "score objects" that increase your score. Much like Neon White, you earn medals based on your score at the end, so there's an incentive to keep playing for a higher score.

The twist is that the whole time you're running around, a clock is constantly counting down. When the clock reaches 0, the game takes away one of your powers. If you've acquired a special power up, it takes that away first. But if you have no special power ups, it starts taking away core aspects of your moveset (e.g., your ability to jump, your ability to run, etc.). If the clock reaches 0 when all of your moveset is gone, you game over.
